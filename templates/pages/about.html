Shrikant Verma Trust

The Shrikant Verma Charitable Trust was established in 1987 in memory of the late Indian poet, writer, and politician Shrikant Verma. The trust was founded by his family to honor his legacy and support initiatives in arts, literature, education, and community welfare.
  • Purpose: To honor the legacy of Shrikant Verma by supporting his vision for society, which involved advancing arts, literature, journalism, and community welfare.
  • Establishment: Founded in 1989 by his son, Abhishek Verma.
  • Activities:

    Supporting arts, literature, and journalism
    Promoting education and community welfare
    Providing financial assistance to needy individuals
  • Public Involvement: The trust and its family members, such as Anca Verma, represent the family at public events, emphasizing themes of empowerment and cultural heritage.
